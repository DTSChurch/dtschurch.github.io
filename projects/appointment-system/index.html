Appointment System

Rock RMS appointment booking and scheduling plugin

Appointment System extends Rock RMS with a complete appointment booking workflow: configurable appointment types, recurring schedules with slot management, public-facing booking with Lava-customizable templates, and automated confirmation and reminder communications.

Rock RMS v17+ .NET Framework 4.7.2 Obsidian (Vue 3) Lava Templates Multi-campus

Who It Serves

Administrators

Create appointment types, configure schedules with slot capacity and duration, set booking thresholds, and manage confirmation and reminder communications.

Public Users

Browse available appointment types, select dates and times, provide contact information, and receive confirmation emails with appointment details.

Staff

View appointment calendars, manage bookings, track attendance status, and process cancellations or reschedules with automated notifications.

System Model

[Placeholder: Entity relationship diagram]

Appointment Type

The root configuration entity that defines a bookable appointment category with campus, location, schedules, booking rules, and communication templates.

Appointment Schedule

Recurring iCal-based schedules with configurable slot capacity, duration subdivisions, buffer times, and category organization.

Appointment Contact

Staff contacts associated with an appointment type who receive notifications about bookings and changes.

Appointments

Individual booked appointments tied to a person, schedule, date/time, and attendance status (Pending, Attended, Cancelled).

Booking Flow

Step 1

Select Appointment Type

[Placeholder: Screenshot of appointment type cards]

Users browse available appointment types displayed as customizable Lava-rendered cards. Optional campus and location filters narrow results. Types with no available slots are visually dimmed.

Step 2

Select Date & Time

[Placeholder: Screenshot of date accordion with time slots]

Available dates are grouped in an accordion with Lava-customizable time slot cards. Each slot shows availability count with color-coded urgency indicators.

Step 3

Confirm & Book

[Placeholder: Screenshot of person information form]

Users enter contact information, fill in any appointment-type-specific attributes, and confirm the booking. Existing Rock persons are matched automatically.

Primary Blocks

Block What It Covers Typical Use
Book Appointment Public-facing multi-step booking flow with Lava-customizable cards, date/time selection, and person matching. External page for members and visitors to schedule appointments.
Appointment Type List Grid of all appointment types with campus, location, and active status. Admin browsing and managing appointment type configurations.
Appointment Type Detail Full configuration: schedules (card picker), campus, location, communications, workflows, and booking rules. Creating or editing an appointment type with all its associated settings.
Appointment Schedule List Grid of all appointment schedules with recurrence and slot details. Managing recurring schedule definitions used by appointment types.
Appointment Schedule Detail iCal schedule configuration, slot capacity, duration subdivisions, and buffer times. Creating or editing a schedule with its recurrence pattern and slot settings.
Appointment List Grid of booked appointments with person, type, date, and attendance status. Staff reviewing and managing individual appointment bookings.
Appointment Detail Individual appointment view with person info, schedule, status, and available dates for rescheduling. Viewing or editing a specific appointment booking.
Appointment Contact List Staff contacts associated with an appointment type. Managing who receives notifications for a given appointment type.
Appointment Contact Detail Individual contact assignment with person picker and admin flag. Adding or editing a staff contact for an appointment type.
Appointment Calendar Calendar and list views of appointments with filtering by type, campus, and category. PDF export support. Staff reviewing appointment schedules across date ranges.

Jobs & Automation

Job Purpose
Send Appointment Reminder Communication Sends reminder emails/SMS X days before appointments. Configurable day intervals. Excludes cancelled appointments.
Send Appointment Completed Notification Sends follow-up communications for appointments that have passed, filtered by attendance status (Pending or Attended).
