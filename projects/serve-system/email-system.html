Workflow

Email system (confirmation + notification)

Serve System sends confirmation emails to volunteers and notification emails to coordinators using Rock System Communications. Both flows share the same sign-up event, but they diverge in recipient resolution, merge fields, and content rules.

System Communications Lava merge fields Campus-aware staff contacts Role/category template matching

Setup Checklist

1. Staff Contacts

Assign a default role contact and optional campus-specific overrides on Serve Opportunity Detail. The most specific contact becomes the sender and merge-field contact.

2. System Communications

Select confirmation and notification templates on the opportunity. The plugin ships with a default Serve Sign-Up Confirmation communication.

3. Content Templates

Optional confirmation content blocks can vary by role, role category, and campus and are rendered into {{ ContentTemplate }}.

4. Notification Recipients

Add people or groups that should receive alerts, optionally filtering them by campus, role, or both.

Delivery Flow

1

Volunteer completes sign-up

The sign-up service assembles the selected role, campus, shifts, and related opportunity context for both outbound flows.

Role Campus ShiftDetails
2

Confirmation path resolves staff contact

The plugin selects the most specific role contact, preferring campus-specific contacts before the role default.

3

Optional content template is matched

Confirmation content is rendered from the most specific template that matches the volunteer’s role, role category, and campus.

4

Notification recipients are filtered

People and groups configured on the opportunity are filtered against the sign-up’s campus and role before notifications are built.

5

System Communications are sent

Confirmation goes to the volunteer immediately. Notification emails are grouped by opportunity context and sent to matching staff and coordinators.

Confirmation Emails

When Sent

Immediately after a volunteer signs up.

Who Receives It

The volunteer who completed the sign-up.

Sender Resolution

The default communication uses the resolved staff contact so replies route to the right ministry owner.

Sender Fields

Field Default Value Purpose
From Email {{ StaffContact.Person.Email }} Sends from the staff contact email so replies route to the assigned contact.
From Name {{ StaffContact.Name }} Displays the resolved staff contact name in the message header.

If no staff contact is configured for the role and campus, Rock falls back to the System Communication default sender or the organization-level email settings.

Template Matching Priority

1

Role + Campus

Most specific match, such as Usher at Northwest.

2

Role Only

Applies across campuses when a role-specific template exists.

3

Role Category + Campus

Useful for ministry-area patterns that still vary by campus.

4

Role Category Only

Applies to all roles in the category when no more specific template exists.

5

Campus Only

Use when any role at a campus should share the same instructions.

6

Default

No role, category, or campus restriction. This is the universal fallback.

Notification Emails

When Sent

After sign-ups are processed and grouped by opportunity context.

Who Receives Them

People or groups configured as notification recipients on the Serve Opportunity.

Template Scope

Notification emails do not receive {{ ContentTemplate }}; that field is confirmation-only.

Recipient Filtering

Recipient Campus Role Receives Notifications For
Sarah — Auditorium Team All Auditorium Team sign-ups across every campus.
John Northwest — All Northwest sign-ups, regardless of role.
Maria Northwest Kids Check-In Only Kids Check-In sign-ups at Northwest.
Admin Group — — Everything for the opportunity.

Troubleshooting

Confirmation Emails Not Sending

Verify ConfirmationSystemEmailId, confirm the System Communication is active, review Rock Communication History, and check SMTP transport settings.

Wrong Sender

Confirm a staff contact exists for the role and campus and that the communication still uses {{ StaffContact.Person.Email }} and {{ StaffContact.Name }}.

Missing Content Template

Check the template priority rules, add a default fallback template, and make sure the communication body includes {{ ContentTemplate }}.

Notification Emails Not Sending

Verify a notification template is selected, at least one recipient exists, the filters match the sign-up, and group recipients still have active members.
